Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking a demo.

Do I need a website to use Mia?

No. Mia can be added to your existing website, or you can choose our Website + Mia plan if your practice needs an informational website.

Will Mia replace my front desk staff?

No. Mia helps answer common questions, collect appointment requests, and support your front desk so your staff can focus on patients.

Can Mia work after office hours?

Yes. Mia is available 24/7 and can capture patient inquiries even when your office is closed.

How long does setup take?

Most practices are up and running within a few business days after we receive your office information.

Does the website include patient portals or online records?

No. The Website + Mia plan includes a professional informational dental website designed to showcase your practice, answer patient questions, generate leads, and support appointment requests.

Where can I see the website options?

You can view our demo website styles on the Website Templates page. The Website + Mia plan can start from one of these styles, then we can adjust the colors, text, services, office details, and Mia setup to better match your practice.

Do you design logos?

Website setup includes simple logo placement using your existing logo. If your practice does not have a logo, we can create a clean text-based website logo for your site. Full custom logo design, branding packages, and unlimited logo revisions are not included.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract required.

How do I receive new appointment requests?

Mia can notify your office by email and text message whenever a patient submits an appointment request.

Can I cancel an annual plan?

Yes. You can cancel renewal at any time. Annual plans are prepaid and discounted, so they are intended to cover the full annual term once service begins.
