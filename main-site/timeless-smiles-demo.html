Meet The Doctor

Meet Dr. Itamar Carter

Dr. Itamar Carter is a highly skilled and compassionate dentist dedicated to providing personalized care for every patient. He began his career as a dental hygienist, gaining invaluable experience in patient care before earning his Doctorate in Dental Medicine from Case Western School of Dental Medicine in Cleveland, Ohio.

Dr. Carter continues to pursue advanced training through a General Practice Residency at Brookdale Hospital in Brooklyn, NY, where he was honored with the prestigious Resident of the Year Award.

With a strong passion for restorative, aesthetics, and implant dentistry, Dr. Carter is committed to staying on the cutting edge of modern dental technology and treatment methods.
