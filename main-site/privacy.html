Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Dos Tiris LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website, forms, chat widgets, and demo requests.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including your name, phone number, email address, practice information, and messages submitted through our website, chat assistant, or demo request forms.

For dental office customers and authorized staff, we may also collect business contact information, including staff name, dental practice name, office email address, and office staff mobile number for account setup, support, and internal alert notifications.

How We Use Information

We use this information to respond to inquiries, provide demos, send requested information, operate Mia, send appointment requests or lead details to the dental office, support customer accounts, and communicate about Dos Tiris services.

SMS Communications

Dos Tiris LLC does not send SMS messages to patients.

Patients communicate with Mia through the website chat. If a patient submits contact information through Mia, that information is sent to the participating dental office so the office can follow up directly.

Dos Tiris LLC may send internal SMS lead alerts only to authorized dental office staff who have opted in to receive those alerts. These alerts may include new website chat inquiries, appointment request details, patient contact details submitted through Mia, and urgent lead notifications.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Authorized dental office staff may reply STOP to opt out at any time and HELP for assistance. Consent to receive internal SMS alerts is not a condition of purchase or service.

No Sharing for Marketing

Mobile information, including mobile phone numbers collected for internal SMS alert purposes, will not be shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

SMS opt-in data and consent for internal SMS alerts will not be shared with any third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information submitted through our website and services.
